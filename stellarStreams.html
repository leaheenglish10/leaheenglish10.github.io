Stellar Streams

AAS Chambliss Finalist 2025



Dancing Stellar Streams in Merging Dark Matter Halos

Stellar Streams are groups of stars orbiting a galaxy's outer edges and are likely formed via globular clusters or dwarf galaxies that have been tidally stripped. My work analyzes simulations of stellar streams in a satellite dark matter halo merging with a Milky Way-like dark matter halo. The movie above shows four of these simulations with varying properties of the satellite halo, exploring mass ratio and orbit.


Stellar Stream N-Body Production Code

In addition, I compare different particle spray methods of modelling stellar streams to help compare to the accuracy of a new stellar stream production code
